**Notes:**

- The top-level `/settings/` route uses `SettingsWrapper` as its component, which directly renders `<Layout.Page>` wrapping an `<Outlet />`. Every child route at any depth therefore inherits Layout.Page.
- None of the intermediate layout wrappers (accountSettingsLayout, accountSecurityWrapper, projectSettingsLayout, organizationSettingsLayout) add their own Layout.Page — they all defer to SettingsWrapper.
- Leaf components (accountDetails, notificationSettingsController, organizationGeneralSettings, etc.) do not use Layout.Page directly — they rely on inheritance.
- Several files referenced in routes.tsx do not exist in the OSS repo (likely getsentry-only): `sentry/views/settings/account/accountSecurity/sessionHistory`, `sentry/views/settings/project/preprod`, `sentry/views/settings/organizationDeveloperSettings/sentryApplicationDashboard`, `sentry/views/settings/organizationTeams/teamSettings`. These are assumed compliant via SettingsWrapper inheritance.
- Stats routes under `/settings/:orgId/stats/` are defined as children of orgSettingsChildren and rendered inside SettingsWrapper, so they are also inherited-compliant.
